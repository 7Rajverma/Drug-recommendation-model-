Building a Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App

Flask, Machine Learning, and a Commitment to Healthcare Innovation

About the Project

In the age of technology, access to accurate and timely healthcare is critical. This project aims to empower users with a web app that predicts potential illnesses based on their symptoms.

The Problem

People often struggle to differentiate between diseases due to overlapping symptoms. A quick, reliable, and user-friendly solution was needed to address this challenge.

The Solution

The app allows users to input symptoms, utilizes a trained Decision Tree Classifier model, and provides accurate disease predictions along with educational resources.

Key Features
  • User-Friendly Interface: Intuitive design ensures users can input symptoms effortlessly.
  • Accurate Predictions: The Decision Tree Classifier provides precise predictions based on symptoms.
  • Comprehensive Information: Offers disease descriptions, precautions, medications, diet, and workout advice.
  • Educational Content: Includes health-focused blog posts to empower users with knowledge.
Conclusion

This project highlights the fusion of technology and healthcare, providing users with a reliable and accessible resource for understanding their health. By leveraging machine learning, we are making strides toward a brighter and healthier future for all.
